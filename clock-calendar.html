Calendar appointments

This requires Evolution to be installed in your computer.

Most distros have come with Evolution installed by default. If yours does not, you may need to install it using your distros package manager.

To view your appointments:

  1. Click on the clock on the top bar.

  2. Click the date for which you want to see your appointments on the Calendar.

  3. The calendar will display existing appointments on the right. As appointments are added in Evolution, they will appear in the clock's appointment list.

To quickly get to the full Evolution calendar, click on the clock and click Open Calendar.

This will work only if you have an existing Evolution account. Otherwise, a window will appear with the necessary steps for adding your first account.
