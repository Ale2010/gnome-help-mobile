Remove a connection between Bluetooth devices

If you don't want to be connected to a Bluetooth device any more, remove the connection:

  1. Click the Bluetooth icon in the top bar, and choose Bluetooth Settings.

  2. Select the device you want to disconnect in the left pane, and then click the - icon underneath the list.

  3. Click Remove.

You can reconnect a Bluetooth device later if desired.
