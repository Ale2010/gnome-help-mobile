Change your timezone

  1. Click on the clock located in the middle of the top bar.

  2. Select Date and Time Settings.

  3. You may need to click Unlock and type the admin password.

  4. Click on your the location on the map, then select your current city from the drop-down list.

The time shown on the top bar will update automatically when you select a different location. The time in the window will be updated next time you access the Date and Time Settings window. You may also wish to set the clock manually.
